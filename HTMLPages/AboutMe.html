Junior Web Developer

When I first moved to the United States from South Korea, I did not know a lick of English other than a single sentence: "I don't speak English." In the hopes of getting my brother and I assimilated and adjusted as quickly as possible, my parents had intentionally moved to an area away from Los Angeles where most Koreans in Southern California reside. With the help of one Korean friend in my class, I was able to quickly learn a new language.

Almost 20 years later, I am yet again in an almost identical situation. I am currently immersed in the process of learning a new language. Actually, several new languages. In about two months time, I hope to be fluent in JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and Node.js among others. Through my blog and this website, I will post updates on my progress. I hope you will join me in my journey to become a professional web developer. Thanks!
